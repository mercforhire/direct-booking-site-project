<objective>
Close the two primary integration gaps: (1) the webhook sends no confirmation email after a date change top-up payment; (2) the guest returning from Stripe with ?date_change_paid=1 sees no success state.

Purpose: Guests who pay for a date change top-up have no feedback that it worked. The webhook silently updates the DB. The ?date_change_paid=1 param is produced by approveDateChange but never consumed by the page.
Output: Webhook sends BookingDateChangePaidEmail; page handles fallback + renders success banner + PAID date change state.
</objective>
